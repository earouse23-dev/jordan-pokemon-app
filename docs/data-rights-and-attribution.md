# Data rights and attribution

- Never scrape TCGplayer, Cardmarket, eBay, Card Ladder, or other marketplaces.
- Provider terms and a commercial-use review are release gates, not post-launch work.
- Store canonical facts separately from provider IDs, quotes, images, and URLs.
- Every quote retains provider, product ID, currency, region, condition, finish, grade context, type, observed/retrieved times, URL, attribution, derivation, and quality.
- Do not persist provider responses or images beyond permitted terms. Cache duration is provider configuration, not a global constant.
- An active listing is an asking price. It is never labeled sold, sale, realized value, or last sold.
- Card imagery should come from an approved catalog/image provider and retain required attribution/outbound links.
- Product copy must state independence from rights holders and marketplaces.

Open unknowns: commercial image redistribution, long-term snapshot retention, affiliate eligibility, and licensed graded/sales-history coverage.
