# Opportunity summary

Collectors lose trust and time when a scan silently chooses the wrong printing, a portfolio mixes incompatible prices, or a spreadsheet cannot show provenance. Mica turns one physical card into a confirmed, editable record and preserves why a value exists—or why it does not.

Primary customer: an active Pokémon collector with 100–5,000 cards who buys periodically, tracks condition and cost, and researches value without running a dealer storefront.

Core opportunity: combine a camera-first intake flow, human confirmation, dense ownership ledger, and source-transparent valuation. Retention comes from repeat acquisition, periodic price refresh, collection cleanup, and research before buying/selling.

Success metrics: median time to confirmed record, top-3 candidate acceptance, correction rate, first-card completion, weekly returning collectors, priced coverage, inline-edit success, provider error rate, and export success.
