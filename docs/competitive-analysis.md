# Competitive analysis

This is directional product research, not a claim about every current feature or price tier.

| Product category | Strength to understand | Opportunity for Mica | Pattern not to copy |
|---|---|---|---|
| Card Ladder | Research depth and historical context | Pokémon-first capture-to-record workflow and explicit capability gaps | Branding, proprietary sales history, or exact layouts |
| TCGplayer | Marketplace liquidity and catalog/condition vocabulary | Neutral ownership ledger with multiple attributable sources | Treating active listings as completed sales |
| Collectr | Fast mobile portfolio feedback | Denser ownership metadata and confidence/freshness detail | Finance-like spectacle or unexplained totals |
| PriceCharting | Broad price lookup and trend research | Printing/variant confirmation before valuation | Conflating non-comparable conditions |
| Dex/binder apps | Satisfying completion and card-image browsing | Professional ledger speed with tactile card emphasis | Pokédex/game-interface trade dress |

Mica’s position is “research ledger”: faster than a spreadsheet, more transparent than a generic portfolio tile, and honest about unsupported pricing.
